Student Management System - Usage Guide

This guide explains how to add, edit, and delete student information using the Student Management System.

1. Adding a Student

  • Click on the Students button and then click on the "Add Students" section.
  • Fill in the Student Name, Class, teacher notes, subjects and hit enter.
  • Click the "Add Student" button to save the student in the database.
Example: Ahmed Al-Rawi, Class 10, Math & Science, Grade: 95

2. Editing a Student

  • Locate the student you want to update in the student table.
  • Click the cell that has the editable info.
  • Update the information such as Name, teacher notes, or exam results.
  • Click anywhere on the screen to apply changes.
Example: Changing Grade from 85 to 90 for Sara Hussein.

3. Deleting a Student

  • Find the student you wish to remove in the table.
  • Click the "-" button (light blue button) next to their record.
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted. The student will be permanently removed from the database.
Example: Deleting Omar Khalid from the system.

Additional Tips

  • You can toggle between Pink Mode and Blue Mode for better visibility.
  • You can toggle the background animations for better control.
  • Always ensure the MySQL server is running locally via XAMPP before performing CRUD operations.
  • Use the search or filter options to quickly find specific students or subjects.

For more details, refer to the README.md and schema.sql files in the project.
